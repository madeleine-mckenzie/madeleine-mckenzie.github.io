Hi!

I'm Madeleine, an Australian astrophysicist interested in all things Milky Way. I was born and raised in Perth, Western Australia, and completed my undergraduate degree and Masters of Astrophysics at the University of Western Australia and International Centre for Radio Astronomy Research. My Masters thesis is titled "Simulating the Formation of Multiple Stellar Populations in Globular Clusters", supervised by Professor Kenji Bekki. I am currently a PhD candidate at the Australian National University, supervised by Dr. David Yong and Professor Mark Krumholz. I primarily work on high precision spectroscopy and isotopic analysis of globular clusters. I am in my final year of my PhD and am currently applying for postdoc positions.
